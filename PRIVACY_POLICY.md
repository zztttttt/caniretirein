# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## 1. Information We Collect

Can I Retire In? is designed with your privacy in mind. All data you enter into the app, including:

- Financial transactions (income and expenses)
- Budget and savings goals
- Net worth information
- Stock and cryptocurrency portfolio data
- Retirement calculator inputs

is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal financial data on our servers.

## 2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage). Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

- **Finnhub API**: Used to fetch stock prices. You may optionally provide your own API key. If you use the demo key, it has strict rate limits. We do not store or transmit your API key.
- **CoinGecko API**: Used to fetch cryptocurrency prices. This is a public API and does not require authentication.

When you use these services, your requests go directly from your device to the respective API providers. We do not intercept or store this data.

## 4. Data Export

You can export your data to CSV or JSON format at any time. This export is performed entirely on your device, and you control where and how you share the exported data.

## 5. Permissions

This app does not require any special permissions. It does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive device data.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing or your preferred method of communication.

## 9. Disclaimer

This app is for educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended to provide financial, tax, or legal advice. All calculations and estimates are approximate and should not be relied upon for making financial decisions.
