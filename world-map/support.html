Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my data?

In the Financial Tracker, click the "Export Data" button to download all your transactions, budgets, and goals as a CSV file for backup.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes! All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your personal information on our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

How do I update stock prices?

Stock prices refresh automatically every 5 minutes when the app is open. You can also manually refresh by clicking the "Refresh Prices" button in the Portfolio Tracker.

Can I use multiple currencies?

Yes! The Retirement Calculator supports multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, CHF, CNY, INR, MXN, BRL, ZAR, and SGD.

How do I set up a budget?

In the Financial Tracker, use the "Budget & Goals" card to create a budget. Select a timeframe (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly) and link it to a category. The app will track your spending against the budget automatically.

Why are my stock prices not updating?

Stock prices require an internet connection. If you're having issues, try adding a free Finnhub API key in the Portfolio Tracker settings for more reliable price updates.

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is important to us. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.
